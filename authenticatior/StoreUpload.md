## 1. Store Listing Details

**Title:** Authenticator Pro: Secure 2FA & TOTP Manager
**Short Description (max 132 chars):** 
Secure, offline 2FA & TOTP manager. Sync across devices, scan QR codes, and protect your accounts with a Master Password.

**Detailed Description:**
Authenticator Pro is a professional-grade, highly secure Two-Factor Authentication (2FA / TOTP) extension designed to protect your digital life. Built with a sleek, modern interface inspired by industry-leading security apps, Authenticator Pro makes managing your 2-step verification codes easier and safer than ever.

🌟 **Key Features:**
- **Bank-Level Security:** Your secret keys are encrypted locally using AES-GCM encryption. Nobody (not even us) can access your codes without your Master Password.
- **Auto-Lock Vault:** The extension securely locks itself when you close your browser. 
- **Offline & Local-First:** Works entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Chrome Sync.
- **Smart QR Code Scanner:** Easily add accounts by scanning QR code images directly from your computer, including bulk imports from Google Authenticator export QR codes.
- **Cross-Device Sync (Optional):** Securely sync your encrypted 2FA vault across all your Chrome browsers using Google Chrome Sync.
- **One-Click Copy:** Quickly copy your 6-digit verification codes with a single click.
- **Data Export & Import:** Easily backup your vault to an encrypted JSON file and restore it anywhere.
- **Bilingual Support:** Fully supports English and Vietnamese languages.
- **Dark & Light Themes:** Beautifully crafted themes that are easy on the eyes.

Protect your accounts from hackers and phishing attacks. Download Authenticator Pro today and take control of your digital security!

## 2. Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

**1. Data Collection & Storage**
Authenticator Pro ("the Extension") is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information, secret keys, or authentication codes on our servers. All data is processed and stored locally on your device or via your personal Google Chrome Sync account (if enabled by you).

**2. Encryption**
All TOTP secret keys are encrypted locally using AES-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard) combined with PBKDF2 key derivation. Your Master Password is used to encrypt this data and is never saved in plain text. If you lose your Master Password, your data cannot be recovered.

**3. Permissions**
The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:
- `storage`: Required to save your encrypted 2FA accounts and user settings.
- `session`: Required to temporarily keep your vault unlocked while the browser remains open.
- `clipboardWrite`: Required to allow you to copy your 2FA codes with a single click.

**4. Third-Party Access**
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or telemetry tools. Your data remains 100% yours.

**5. Changes to this Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the Chrome Web Store listing.
